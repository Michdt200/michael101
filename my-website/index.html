About Me

Hello! I'm Michael Dashur, a dedicated and enthusiastic web designer based in Lagos, Nigeria. Born on July 10, 2007, I've always been drawn to the creative and technical aspects of web development. At 17, I am proud to have graduated from Mare Secondary School and currently further my education with an Advanced Diploma in Software Engineering (ADSE) at Aptech Nigeria. My journey into the world of web design began early, driven by a fascination with how websites can blend aesthetics with functionality. I specialize in using HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to craft visually appealing and user-friendly websites. My designs aim to provide seamless user experiences while incorporating modern trends and responsive design principles. Being Nigerian is a significant part of my identity, and I enjoy infusing my cultural background into my projects, creating unique and meaningful digital experiences. I am passionate about learning and continually strive to expand my knowledge, staying updated with the latest advancements in web technology. Through my portfolio, I hope to showcase not just my technical skills but also my commitment to innovation and excellence in design. I look forward to connecting with like-minded individuals and contributing to exciting projects that make a difference.
